### **How do you handle the complexity of multi-state payroll and local tax withholdings?**

Navigating the "patchwork" of US state and local taxes is one of the biggest risks for your clients. Our team is specialized in multi-state compliance, ensuring that SUI, SDI, and local occupational taxes are calculated with precision across all 50 states. We monitor nexus changes and local rate updates proactively, so you don't have to worry about your clients receiving late-payment notices or IRS penalties.

### **Can you work within our existing payroll platforms like Gusto or ADP?**

Yes. We are tech-agnostic and operate as "power users" in the systems you already trust. Whether your clients use **Gusto, Rippling, ADP Workforce Now, or QuickBooks Payroll**, we plug directly into those environments. We handle the gross-to-net calculations, garnishment management, and benefit deductions natively within your stack, ensuring a seamless data flow to the general ledger.

### **How do you ensure payroll is processed on time and without errors?**

We utilize a rigid checklist-driven process tailored to each client’s pay schedule. Every payroll run undergoes a dual-verification check—matching hours, rates, and deductions against the previous period to catch anomalies before the "submit" button is ever hit. Because we leverage global time zones, we can often finalize calculations overnight, giving you ample time for a final review before the direct deposit deadline.

### **What about year-end compliance, such as W-2s and 1099s?**

Year-end is where our support truly shines. We handle the heavy lifting of reconciling quarterly 941s to the annual 940 and generating W-2 and 1099-NEC forms. We ensure that fringe benefits, taxable commissions, and health insurance premiums are correctly recorded, delivering a clean, audit-ready package to your tax team long before the January 31st deadline.
