Section 3: The Use Cases Cards

Case 01: Demand Forecasting Engine

The Problem: High inventory carrying costs and stockouts due to fragmented regional data.

Tools & Tech: Azure ML, Power BI, Dynamics 365 Supply Chain.

The Approach: Developed region-level predictive models that pull real-time signals from ERP and external market data.

Business Impact: 25% improvement in forecast accuracy and a 12% reduction in overstock within 6 months.

Case 02: Executive Decision Dashboards (Data Modernization)

The Problem: Leadership was making decisions based on "month-old" PDF reports and siloed spreadsheets.

Tools & Tech: Power BI Premium, Azure Synapse, DAX.

The Approach: Centralized 14 disparate data sources into a single Azure Lakehouse with automated KPI refreshes every 4 hours.

Business Impact: 90% elimination of manual reporting hours and real-time visibility into global margin leakage.

Case 03: Customer Analytics & Segmentation

The Problem: Generic marketing spend with low ROI due to an inability to identify high-value "at-risk" customers.

Tools & Tech: Azure AI, Dynamics 365 Customer Insights, Python.

The Approach: ML-driven segmentation using RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) modeling and predictive Lifetime Value (LTV) scoring.

Business Impact: 15% increase in customer retention and highly targeted personalization at scale.

Case 04: Supply Chain Optimization (The "Agentic" Angle)

The Problem: Procurement teams were reactive, only discovering vendor delays after they impacted production.

Tools & Tech: Azure Databricks, AI Agents, Dynamics 365 SCM.

The Approach: An "Agentic" monitoring system that scans global risk signals and vendor history to predict bottlenecks before they occur.

Business Impact: 18% reduction in inventory holding costs and a 22% improvement in on-time delivery.

Case 05: Enterprise Knowledge Assistant (GenAI + RAG)

The Problem: Support and Sales teams were spending 2+ hours a day searching through thousands of internal PDFs, Sharepoint folders, and technical manuals to answer client queries.

Tools & Tech: Azure OpenAI (GPT-4), Pinecone (Vector DB), LangChain.

The Approach: Built a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) "Knowledge Engine" that indexes all unstructured company data with enterprise-grade security and citation tracking.

Business Impact: 80% reduction in document search time and a 30% improvement in first-call resolution for technical support teams.

Case 06: Automated Financial Reconciliation

The Problem: Finance teams were manually matching bank statements to ERP invoices across 12 international entities, leading to month-end closing delays and human error.

Tools & Tech: Python (AI-matching logic), Power Automate, Dynamics 365 Finance.

The Approach: An Intelligent Automation layer that uses fuzzy matching and ML to reconcile 95% of transactions automatically, flagging only high-risk anomalies for human review.

Business Impact: Month-end close shortened by 4 days and 100% audit-readiness with automated transaction logging.
