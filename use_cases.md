Case 01: Demand Forecasting Engine

- __The Problem:__ High inventory carrying costs and stockouts due to fragmented regional data\.
- __Tools & Tech:__ Azure ML, Power BI, Dynamics 365 Supply Chain\.
- __The Approach:__ Developed region\-level predictive models that pull real\-time signals from ERP and external market data\.
- __Business Impact: 25% improvement__ in forecast accuracy and a 12% reduction in overstock within 6 months\.

Case 02: Executive Decision Dashboards \(Data Modernization\)

- __The Problem:__ Leadership was making decisions based on "month\-old" PDF reports and siloed spreadsheets\.
- __Tools & Tech:__ Power BI Premium, Azure Synapse, DAX\.
- __The Approach:__ Centralized 14 disparate data sources into a single Azure Lakehouse with automated KPI refreshes every 4 hours\.
- __Business Impact:__ __90% elimination of manual reporting hours__ and real\-time visibility into global margin leakage\.

Case 03: Customer Analytics & Segmentation

- __The Problem:__ Generic marketing spend with low ROI due to an inability to identify high\-value "at\-risk" customers\.
- __Tools & Tech:__ Azure AI, Dynamics 365 Customer Insights, Python\.
- __The Approach:__ ML\-driven segmentation using RFM \(Recency, Frequency, Monetary\) modeling and predictive Lifetime Value \(LTV\) scoring\.
- __Business Impact:__ __15% increase in customer retention__ and highly targeted personalization at scale\.

Case 04: Supply Chain Optimization \(The "Agentic" Angle\)

- __The Problem:__ Procurement teams were reactive, only discovering vendor delays after they impacted production\.
- __Tools & Tech:__ Azure Databricks, AI Agents, Dynamics 365 SCM\.
- __The Approach:__ An "Agentic" monitoring system that scans global risk signals and vendor history to predict bottlenecks before they occur\.
- __Business Impact:__ __18% reduction in inventory holding costs__ and a 22% improvement in on\-time delivery\.

Case 05: Enterprise Knowledge Assistant \(GenAI \+ RAG\)

- __The Problem:__ Support and Sales teams were spending 2\+ hours a day searching through thousands of internal PDFs, Sharepoint folders, and technical manuals to answer client queries\.
- __Tools & Tech:__ Azure OpenAI \(GPT\-4\), Pinecone \(Vector DB\), LangChain\.
- __The Approach:__ Built a Retrieval\-Augmented Generation \(RAG\) "Knowledge Engine" that indexes all unstructured company data with enterprise\-grade security and citation tracking\.
- __Business Impact:__ __80% reduction in document search time__ and a 30% improvement in first\-call resolution for technical support teams\.

Case 06: Automated Financial Reconciliation

- __The Problem:__ Finance teams were manually matching bank statements to ERP invoices across 12 international entities, leading to month\-end closing delays and human error\.
- __Tools & Tech:__ Python \(AI\-matching logic\), Power Automate, Dynamics 365 Finance\.
- __The Approach:__ An Intelligent Automation layer that uses fuzzy matching and ML to reconcile 95% of transactions automatically, flagging only high\-risk anomalies for human review\.
- __Business Impact:__ __Month\-end close shortened by 4 days__ and 100% audit\-readiness with automated transaction logging\.
