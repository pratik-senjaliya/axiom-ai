### **H3 1\. Audit Planning & Risk Assessment**

*Focus: Strategic groundwork to ensure a focused and efficient audit.*

* **Pre-Audit Engagement Support:**  
  * **Task:** Rolling forward the previous year’s electronic audit files in software like **CaseWare, AdvanceFlow, or CCH Axcess**.  
  * **Task:** Drafting Engagement Letters and Independence Confirmations for the audit team.  
* **Materiality & Risk Identification:**  
  * **Task:** Calculating **Overall Materiality** and **Performance Materiality** based on the chosen benchmark (e.g., Total Assets or Revenue).  
  * **Task:** Preparing **Planning Analytics** (Flux Analysis) to identify significant account balances and unusual trends.

### **H3 2\. Substantive Testing & Execution**

*Focus: The "Heavy Lifting" of verifying account balances.*

* **Sample Selection & Testing:**  
  * **Task:** Using **IDEA or ACL** to perform statistical and non-statistical sampling.  
  * **Task:** **Vouching:** Tracing samples from the General Ledger to source documents (Invoices, BOLs, Contracts).  
  * **Task:** **Tracing:** Selecting source documents and tracing them into the General Ledger to ensure completeness.  
* **Account-Specific Scrutiny:**  
  * **Task:** **Cash:** Preparing bank reconciliations and performing "Casting" procedures on bank statements.  
  * **Task:** **Accounts Receivable:** Managing the **Confirmation Process** (preparing, sending, and tracking responses via https://www.google.com/search?q=Confirmation.com).  
  * **Task:** **Inventory:** Reviewing physical inventory count sheets and performing "Price Testing" (lower of cost or market).  
  * **Task:** **Search for Unrecorded Liabilities:** Reviewing post-balance sheet payments to ensure proper period cutoff.

### **H3 3\. Internal Control & Compliance Testing**

*Focus: Testing the "Plumbing" of the organization.*

* **Walkthroughs & Narratives:**

  * **Task:** Testing the **Operating Effectiveness** of internal controls (e.g., verifying that all checks over $5,000 have two signatures).

  * **Task:** Verifying adherence to industry-specific regulations (e.g., HIPAA for Healthcare or HUD for Real Estate).
