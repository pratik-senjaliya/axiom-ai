__1\. How is AI used in sustainability and carbon tracking?__

AI helps automate data collection, identify emission patterns, and simulate future scenarios\. It transforms sustainability from static reporting into predictive and actionable intelligence\.

__2\. What are Scope 1, 2, and 3 emissions?__

Scope 1 covers direct emissions, Scope 2 includes purchased energy, and Scope 3 involves supply chain and indirect emissions\. Most organizations struggle primarily with Scope 3 visibility\.
