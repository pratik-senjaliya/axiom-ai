## Frequently Asked Questions

#### 1\. How do we choose between Microsoft Dynamics 365, SAP S/4HANA, and Oracle?

The "right" platform depends on your industry complexity, global footprint, and existing IT ecosystem\. __SAP__ is the gold standard for complex global manufacturing; __Microsoft Dynamics__ offers the best integration for companies already using the Microsoft stack; __Oracle/NetSuite__ is highly efficient for finance\-heavy and high\-growth services\. We provide a __Platform Fit Assessment__ to help you decide based on total cost of ownership \(TCO\) and long\-term scalability\.

#### 2\. Our current ERP is a "mess"—should we clean the data before or during migration?

We recommend a __"Clean as you Migrate"__ approach\. Trying to clean decades of legacy data in an old system is often wasted effort\. We use automated data\-cleansing pipelines to filter, deduplicate, and Map\-Reduce your data as it moves into the new environment, ensuring your new ERP starts with a "Single Source of Truth\."

#### 3\. What is a "Clean Core" strategy, and why is it important?

A "Clean Core" involves keeping the ERP's standard codebase untouched and using external APIs or Low\-Code platforms \(like Power Platform or BTP\) for customizations\. This prevents "Technical Debt," making future software updates instant and ensuring your system remains compatible with new AI features\.

#### 4\. How long does a typical ERP transformation take?

While a full global rollout can take 12–18 months, we advocate for a __Phased Modular Approach\.__ We aim to get your "Core Finance" or "Critical Operations" live in 4–6 months, delivering immediate value while we migrate secondary business units in subsequent waves\.

#### 5\. Can you help us optimize an ERP implementation that has already failed?

Yes\. A large portion of our work involves __ERP Rescue Projects\.__ We conduct a 2\-week diagnostic to identify if the failure is technical \(bad code\), functional \(poor process mapping\), or organizational \(lack of training\), and then build a "Get\-Well" plan to put the project back on track\.

#### 6\. Is modern Cloud ERP really more secure than our On\-Premise servers?

Statistically, yes\. Cloud providers like Microsoft, SAP, and Oracle spend billions annually on cybersecurity that no individual enterprise can match\. We ensure your cloud ERP is configured with __Zero Trust Architecture__, multi\-factor authentication, and automated audit logs to exceed global compliance standards\.
